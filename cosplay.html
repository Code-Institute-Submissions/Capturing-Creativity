What is cosplay?


We go to conventions all over Ireland and talk to the people who attend.


We interview cosplayers about who they're dressed up as, and why they do cosplay.


We take a lot of photographs and videos of the people in attendance, the cosplayers, the creators, and the amazing props.


We have a lot of fun, like you should do at conventions.

Why do people do it?


First and foremost, we love geek culture and the many sub-cultures you can find at conventions.


Cosplay can be simply incredible. The processes, people, and the finished product all deserve to celebrated, and we want to give all of that so much attention.


Comics. Are. Not. A. Genre. Comics (comicbooks, comic strips, manga and so on) are not a genre, but rather a medium, and an important one at that. The people that bring some of our favourite stories to life have voices to be heard.
We specifically push the people in the "small press" section of Irish comics.

About

We at Capturing Creativity are lovers of conventions, comics, and cosplay.
